DF Adventure Mode: Ultimate Guide

Tutorial to DF adventure mode

Alright, so, Reddit has gone insane over adventure mode shit,
so I might as well make a guide of my own

This all comes from personal experience, and testing things both live in adventure mode and in the object testing arena

Myths:

First, I'd like to clear out some confusing myths about combat in adventure mode.

1: Leaves wreck bogeymen?

I would say this is incorrect. Trying to fight bogeymen with leaves is unfortunately not as effective as people make it out to be.

This game simulates "misc. objects" as boxes, which mean while a leaf hit a lot, it won't hit hard with the physics in this game.

They are, however surprisingly strong, with a decent Fighter and Misc. Object User skill.

2: Throwing is op

Yeah, sorta. Throwing rocks and coins can "chip bones" and deal other types of bone wounds.

Bone damage in this game causes huge amounts of pain, and anything without [NOPAIN] will likely give in after throwing enough

3: Necromancer is op

Yep, 100%. The fixed stat is bugged and you can still increase attributes, and althrough minions don't seem strong, the overwhelming amout of them can kill things easily.

Just watch out for AoE attacks, as raised bodyparts tend to cluster together and get all killed

4: Spider/Elephant men are op

Spider men are kind of totally op, because they can wield a million shield and be invincible. However, Elephant men are op againist a lot of things with no armor, if you have a good Fighter/Striker/Kicker/Observer skill, you can really destroy almost anything.
