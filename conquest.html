Conquests

Conquests are long, campaign-style games, in which the entire world is the map and you're free to do what you want.
You can choose from a number of different countries and you have to defeat all enemy nations in 300 turns. Enemy nations are defeated by taking all of their ports and cities.

You earn both medals and resources on your first time completing a conquest, but after that only resources are received. 1-star countries give more resources but are oft harder to win with.

If you are struggling with a conquest, feel free to take a look at the community-created guides on the right side, or the conquest tips.
If you've successfully completed a conquest that doesn't have a guide yet, let the world know how you did it! Many people appreciate guides, as conquests are the easiest way to earn resources without overly grinding.
