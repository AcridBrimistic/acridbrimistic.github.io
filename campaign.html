Campaigns

Campaigns are short missions, usually lasting around 20-30 turns to complete. There are two difficulties for campaigns, normal and hard. Hard missions are much more difficult than their normal counterparts, as the enemy is stronger and there are extra objectives. Campaigns reward the plyer with medals and resources.
